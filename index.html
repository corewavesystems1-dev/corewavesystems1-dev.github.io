Information Collection and Use

Pizza Puzzle is a standalone, offline puzzle game. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) or personal data from our users.

All game progress, settings, levels, and high scores are saved locally on your device only using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

We do not require you to create an account, log in, provide your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal information to use the App.

2

Automatically Collected Information

While we do not actively collect any data, the App distribution platform (Google Play Store) and your device's operating system may automatically collect certain diagnostic and usage data as governed by their respective privacy policies. This may include:

📊
App performance metrics and crash reports (via Google Play Console)
📱
Device model, OS version, and basic diagnostics
📥
App install and uninstall statistics
🔋
Battery and network usage statistics

We do not have access to personally identifiable data through these platform services. This data is handled entirely by Google as per their Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services and SDKs

Currently, Pizza Puzzle does not integrate any of the following third-party services:

❌
No analytics SDKs (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics)
❌
No advertising frameworks (e.g., AdMob, Unity Ads)
❌
No social media SDKs (e.g., Facebook SDK)
❌
No tracking or user profiling tools

If we introduce any such services in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly, and any data collection will be fully compliant with Google Play Console Developer Program Policies.

4

Data Retention and Deletion

Because we do not collect or store your personal data on any servers, there is no data for us to retain, process, or delete on our end.

To delete your locally stored game data: Go to your device's Settings → Apps → Pizza Puzzle → Storage → Clear Data. Alternatively, you can simply uninstall the App from your device.

Once game data is cleared or the App is uninstalled, all locally stored game progress, high scores, and level data will be permanently and irreversibly deleted.

🇪🇺

👨‍👩‍👧
Google Play Families Policy — For apps used by children

No Ads, No In-App Purchases, No External Links visible to children

📍

📷

Security

We value your trust in using our App. Since no personal information is transmitted over the internet or stored on external servers by us, the risk of data breaches, unauthorized access, or data theft related to our App is virtually non-existent.

Your game data is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's own security mechanisms (screen lock, encryption, etc.).
